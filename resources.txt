Title: FourBlock

Link: https://fourblock.org/
Desc: FourBlock is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting returning service members in their transition from military service to meaningful civilian careers. Our mission is to build a nationwide community that all transitioning veterans and service members may connect with wherever they are locally to gain access to the career development resources, industry exploration, and professional network they need to find their new purpose and transition successfully into the right careers.
